Products

Premium exotic fruits for wholesale and export

Rambutan

Sweet, juicy, and highly sought after in retail and hotel channels. Ideal for seasonal bulk buying.

  • Season: May–August
  • Packaging: 5 kg / 10 kg cartons, crates
  • Suitability: Retail, supermarkets, export lots

Butter Fruit (Avocado)

High-value fruit with strong demand in foodservice and health-focused retail segments.

  • Season: Year-round
  • Packaging: 4 kg / 10 kg cartons
  • Suitability: Hotels, HORECA, exporters

Mangosteen

Premium tropical fruit known for its delicate flavor and premium presentation.

  • Season: June–September
  • Packaging: Crates and ventilated cartons
  • Suitability: High-end retail and export packs

Durian

Luxury fruit with strong demand in specialty import markets and premium hospitality.

  • Season: June–August
  • Packaging: Custom crates with ventilation
  • Suitability: Specialty buyers, exporters
